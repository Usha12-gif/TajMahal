The Moonlit Wonder

Witnessing the ivory-white marble reflecting the celestial glow of a full moon is an ethereal experience. Plan your exclusive night tour parameters below.

Viewing Schedule

Night viewing is allowed strictly 5 nights in a month: on the full moon night, and two nights before and after it (except on Fridays and the month of Ramzan).

Timings & Batches

Authorized access opens from 8:30 PM to 12:30 AM. Visitors are organized into 8 distinct batches of 50 people each, with a max duration of 30 minutes per slot.

Ticketing Policy

Tickets must be booked 24 hours in advance from the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) office. Standard identification details are mandatory for verification.
